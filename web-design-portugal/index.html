Why most sites underperform

Most business websites are an expensive brochure.

It looks fine in a presentation, the logo sits nicely, everyone signs it off - and then it sits there for three years without bringing a single customer. The problem is almost never the design. It's that the site was built as a digital leaflet instead of a sales tool: slow to load, invisible in the searches that matter, and with no idea how many enquiries it produced last month.

We start from the other end. Who the customer is, what they type into Google when they have the problem you solve, and what has to happen on the site for that person to get in touch. The design follows - and it's better for it, not worse.

Frequently asked

The questions we always get.

How much does a website cost in Portugal?

Our projects start at €4,500 and rise with what the site has to do. A well-built marketing site is one project; an online store with integrations and several languages is another. We don't sell fixed packages, because they rarely fit anyone's business. You get the number in the first conversation, before anything starts - and if we think you don't need what you're asking for, we'll say so.

Do you work with businesses outside Lisbon?

Yes - most of our clients are elsewhere. We've delivered work in 15 countries, so a few hundred kilometres within Portugal is nothing. Most of a project runs remotely, and we visit when it's worth it. You get the same senior work whether you're in Lisbon, Porto or a village in the Alentejo.

Can you build the site in English as well as Portuguese?

Yes, and it's one of the things we do most. Each language is written properly rather than machine-translated, with hreflang set up so every edition ranks in its own market instead of competing with the others. If you sell beyond Portugal, this is usually the highest-value part of the project.

How long does a website take?

A marketing site typically takes four to eight weeks, depending on scope and how quickly content and feedback come back. Larger builds with ecommerce or several languages take longer. You get a realistic timeline up front and you see the work as it happens.

Do I own the website and the code?

Yes. All code, domains, accounts and data are in your name. Plenty of agencies keep the site on their own platform so leaving means starting over. With us you take everything with you, including the hosting, which is fast and carries no monthly fee on most projects.

Will my site actually show up on Google?

SEO is built into the foundation, not sold afterwards - structure, speed, structured data and content built around real searches. What we won't do is promise position one by a given date; anyone who does is making it up. What we guarantee is a site built to rank, and dashboards that show you honestly where it stands.
