The Labs Sprint

Six weeks. One shipped workflow. Real ROI.

Our Labs Sprint is a fixed-scope, fixed-fee engagement that takes you from zero to a production AI feature — with evals, dashboards and full team handoff at the end.

Weeks 1–2
Audit & Roadmap

Workflow mapping, ROI scoring, picking the right first bet. You leave with a clear plan and buy-in from stakeholders.

Weeks 3–4
Prototype

A working AI feature integrated with your stack and data — not a mock, not a slide. Something your team can actually use.

Weeks 5–6
Ship & Evaluate

Production launch, evals framework, performance dashboards and team handoff so you can keep iterating after we wrap.
