Design & Brand

Design that starts with your users, ends with results.

Good UX is invisible. Bad UX is why your users drop off at step three. We start every engagement with real research — interviews, journey mapping, friction audits — and build toward interfaces that feel effortless. Prototype-driven, evidence-based, and delivered in Figma so your developers can ship immediately.

What we do

Research, design, validate. Repeat.

User research & journey mapping

Qualitative interviews, usability sessions and analytics analysis translated into clear journey maps. We identify where users succeed, where they stall, and what it would take to tip them into action.

Wireframes & interactive prototypes

Low-fidelity wireframes to validate structure fast, then high-fidelity Figma prototypes that stakeholders and test participants can click through. No more miscommunication between design intent and developer output.

Design system creation

Scalable component libraries built in Figma with proper variants, auto-layout and token documentation. Designed once, used by every team member and every future feature — with full developer handoff baked in.

Our approach

Four stages from ambiguity to shipped design.

01

Research

We run five to eight user interviews, review session recordings, analyse drop-off data and audit competitor UX. The goal is to separate what users say from what users do — the gap is almost always where the design opportunity lives.

02

Define

Research outputs are synthesised into user personas, journey maps and a prioritised problem statement. We run a collaborative workshop with your team to align on the core problems worth solving — before a single frame is drawn.

03

Design

Wireframes first — fast, scrappy, cheap to change. Once structure is agreed we move to high-fidelity screens in Figma, building out the component library as we go. Every component has documented states: default, hover, focus, error, loading.

04

Test

Moderated usability testing with five target users on the Figma prototype. We record, analyse and deliver a usability report with ranked issues and recommended fixes. Design is refined and a clean developer handoff package is produced.

What's included

  • Wireframe set for all key flows
