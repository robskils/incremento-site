Design & Brand

Websites that convert, not just impress.

Most agency sites look great in a Figma presentation and underperform in production. We design from the conversion up — brand-aligned systems that load fast, guide visitors deliberately, and turn browsers into buyers. Trusted by Lisbon-based businesses and international brands alike.

What we do

Three pillars of every engagement.

Brand-aligned design systems

Colour, type, spacing and component libraries that extend consistently across every page and touchpoint. Built once, used everywhere — no visual inconsistency as your site grows.

Conversion-optimised layouts

Page structure informed by scroll depth, heatmap patterns and proven CRO principles. Every section has a job: capture attention, build trust, or trigger action.

Responsive & accessible builds

Pixel-perfect across mobile, tablet and desktop. WCAG 2.1 AA compliant by default — because accessibility and performance are the same conversation.

Our approach

From brief to live in four stages.

01

Discovery

We dig into your business goals, audience and competitive landscape before a single frame is drawn. This means stakeholder interviews, site analytics review (if available), and a competitor visual audit. Output: a shared creative brief and page architecture.

02

Design

We design in Figma — starting with key pages (homepage, primary landing page) and expanding the design system outward. Two revision rounds are included. Every decision is annotated so developers and stakeholders understand the intent, not just the pixels.

03

Build

Clean semantic HTML, CSS and JS (or React/Next.js for more complex sites) delivered through your chosen CMS — Webflow, Sanity, Contentful or plain files. We prioritise Core Web Vitals from day one: LCP, INP and CLS are measured, not crossed fingers.

04

Launch

We handle DNS, CDN configuration, 301 redirects and analytics verification. Post-launch we monitor for 30 days, fix any issues that surface and hand over a full site documentation pack. You own everything.

What's included

Everything you need to go live with confidence.

  • Brand-aligned design system (colours, typography, components)
  • Desktop & mobile designs for all agreed pages (Figma)
  • Clickable prototype for stakeholder sign-off
  • CMS integration & editor training
  • On-page SEO: meta tags, structured data, sitemap, robots.txt
  • GA4 & Google Search Console setup
  • 30-day post-launch monitoring & support
