Design & Brand

Websites that convert, not just impress.

Most agency sites look great in a Figma presentation and underperform in production. We design from the conversion up - brand-aligned systems that load fast, guide visitors deliberately, and turn browsers into buyers. Trusted by Lisbon-based businesses and international brands alike.

What we do

Three pillars of every engagement.

Brand-aligned design systems

Colour, type, spacing and component libraries that extend consistently across every page and touchpoint. Built once, used everywhere - no visual inconsistency as your site grows.

Conversion-optimised layouts

Page structure informed by scroll depth, heatmap patterns and proven CRO principles. Every section has a job: capture attention, build trust, or trigger action.

Responsive & accessible builds

Pixel-perfect across mobile, tablet and desktop. WCAG 2.1 AA compliant by default - because accessibility and performance are the same conversation.

Our approach

From brief to live in four stages.

01

Discovery

We dig into your business goals, audience and competitive landscape before a single frame is drawn. This means stakeholder interviews, site analytics review (if available), and a competitor visual audit. Output: a shared creative brief and page architecture.

02

Design

We design in Figma - starting with key pages (homepage, primary landing page) and expanding the design system outward. Two revision rounds are included. Every decision is annotated so developers and stakeholders understand the intent, not just the pixels.

03

Build

Clean semantic HTML, CSS and JS (or React/Next.js for more complex sites) delivered through your chosen CMS - Webflow, Sanity, Contentful or plain files. We prioritise Core Web Vitals from day one: LCP, INP and CLS are measured, not crossed fingers.

04

Launch

We handle DNS, CDN configuration, 301 redirects and analytics verification. Post-launch we monitor for 30 days, fix any issues that surface and hand over a full site documentation pack. You own everything.

What's included

Everything you need to go live with confidence.

Frequently asked

The questions we always get.

How much does web design cost in Lisbon?

Our projects start at €4,500 and rise with what the site has to do. A well-built marketing site is one project; an ecommerce build with integrations and several languages is another. We don't sell fixed packages, because they rarely fit anyone's business properly. You get the number in the first conversation, before anything starts - and if we think you don't need what you're asking for, we'll say so.

How long does a website take to build?

A marketing site typically takes four to eight weeks, depending on scope and how quickly content and feedback come back. Larger builds with ecommerce, integrations or multiple languages take longer. You get a realistic timeline up front and you see the work as it happens - we don't disappear for a month and reappear with a surprise.

Is SEO included in the web design?

Yes, and it isn't an upsell. Structure, speed, structured data and content built around what people actually search for all go into the foundation. What we won't do is promise you position one by a given date - anyone promising that is making it up. What we guarantee is that the site is built to rank, and that the analytics dashboards show you exactly what it's doing.

Do I own the website and the code?

Yes. All code, domains, accounts and data are in your name. Plenty of agencies hold the site hostage - leave, and you lose everything. With us you take it all with you, including the hosting, which is fast and carries no monthly fee on most projects.

Can you build the site in more than one language?

Yes - it's one of the things we do most. English, Portuguese, Spanish or whatever you need, each edition written properly rather than run through machine translation, with hreflang set up so each one ranks in its own market. Our own site runs in three languages, so you can see the approach before you buy it.

Do you only work with businesses in Lisbon?

No. We're based in Lisbon and we like meeting clients in person when it makes sense, but we've delivered work in 15 countries. A good part of what we do is multilingual precisely because clients are selling beyond Portugal.
